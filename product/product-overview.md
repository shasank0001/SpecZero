# HealthClinic Pro

> Modern healthcare management, simplified.

## Problem Statement

Healthcare practices struggle with fragmented systems for patient management, appointment scheduling, and medical records. Staff spend excessive time on administrative tasks instead of patient care, leading to inefficiencies, scheduling conflicts, and frustrated patients.

## Target Users

- **Practice Administrators** - Need complete visibility into clinic operations, staff scheduling, and financial metrics
- **Healthcare Providers (Doctors/Nurses)** - Require quick access to patient records, appointment schedules, and prescription management
- **Front Desk Staff** - Handle patient check-ins, appointment scheduling, and insurance verification
- **Patients** - Want easy appointment booking, prescription refills, and access to their health records

## Key Features

### Dashboard & Analytics
Real-time overview of practice performance with key metrics including patient counts, appointment statistics, revenue tracking, and activity feeds. Quick actions for common tasks.

### Patient Management
Comprehensive patient records with demographics, medical history, allergies, medications, insurance information, and document storage. Full CRUD operations with search and filtering.

### Appointment Scheduling
Intuitive scheduling system with calendar views, appointment types (in-person, video, phone), status tracking, automated reminders, and conflict detection.

### Settings & Preferences
Customizable user preferences including profile management, notification settings, appearance options, security controls, and billing management.

## Success Metrics

- Reduce appointment scheduling time by 50%
- Decrease patient no-show rates by 30%
- Improve staff productivity by 40%
- Achieve 95% patient satisfaction rating

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js 15, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **Backend:** PostgreSQL, Prisma ORM, Zod validation
- **Auth:** Clerk
